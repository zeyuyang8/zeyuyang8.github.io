Zeyu Yang is a second-year PhD student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Rice University, where he is fortunate to work under the supervision of Dr. Anshumali Shrivastava. His research focuses on efficient AI systems, including quantization, parameter-efficient fine-tuning, and lossless compression. He is also interested in AI agents, including self-evolving, long-horizon planning, and multi-agent collaboration.
